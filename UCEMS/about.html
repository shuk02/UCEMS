Our Story

The UPTM Campus Entrepreneurship Manager (UCEM) aims to streamline to support students diploma and degree enrolled in entrepreneurship subjects at the UPTM. This platform serves as a comprehensive tool for students to manage their business to related activities, while also allowing lecturers to oversee and evaluate their progress. It will includes advanced security features implemented on it.

Mission

To become a leading digital platform that empowers student entrepreneurs in Malaysian universities by fostering innovation, promoting sustainable business practices, and bridging the gap between education and real-world entrepreneurship.

Vision

Our mission is to support and enhance student entrepreneurship by providing a user-friendly, secure, and data-driven management system that enables students to track their business progress, and lecturers to monitor and guide entrepreneurial activities effectively. UCEMS aims to cultivate a culture of innovation and business accountability within the academic environment.
